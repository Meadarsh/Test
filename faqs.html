FAQs

What does Online UU stand for?

Online UU stands for Online Uttaranchal University under the control of the Centre for Distance & Online Education department of the University.

What is Online Learning?

Online Learning, also known as e-Learning, refers to the use of the Internet and Digital technologies to facilitate and enhance the learning process. It provides individuals with the flexibility to access educational materials and pursue in the programs virtually, anywhere at anytime. Online learning encompasses a variety of formats, including video lectures, interactive quizzes, discussion forums, and other multimedia resources.

What are the benefits of Online Learning?

Online Learning makes lifelong learning possible. Along with enhanced access to educational opportunities; it offers convenience, flexibility and affordability. Out of many benefits, some are listed here:

  • Suitable for anyone who wants to pursue higher education from anywhere
  • Self-paced learning which enables the control of personal time
  • No constraint of time and place as the learning is fully online and flexible
  • Multi-tasking opportunity along with pursuing the degree online
  • No Age bar. Learning has no barriers, so the age is not a matter to achieve your UG and PG degree

Where is Uttaranchal University located?

Uttaranchal University is located in Dehradun, Uttarakhand and is well connected by Road, Rail, and Air; having a reasonable distance from the Bus stand, Railway station, and the Airport in Dehradun.

Is Uttaranchal University (UU) recognized by UGC?

Uttaranchal University is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC), a statutory body of the Government of India established for the coordination, determination, and maintenance of standards of university education in India.

Is UU a member of the Association of Indian Universities (AIU)?

Yes, UU is a member of the Association of Indian Universities (AIU).

Is UU recognized for offering Online Learning (OL) Programmes?

Yes, Uttaranchal University is entitled by the University Grants Commission (UGC) to offer Online Degree Programs to the Indian National and International Learners.

Is UU NAAC (National Assessment and Accreditation Council) accredited?

Yes, Uttaranchal University is one of the Top 5% Universities and the first to get the prestigious NAAC A+ accreditation in the Indian State of Uttarakhand.

Are UU programs approved by the AICTE?

Yes, Uttaranchal University's Online MBA and Online MCA degrees are recognized by the AICTE.

Is UU recognized by the WES (World Education Services)?

Yes, WES acknowledges the degrees awarded by Uttaranchal University.

What are the duration of the Programs offered by Online UU?

UG Programs: Minimum 3 years and Maximum 6 Years

PG Programs: Minimum 2 years and Maximum 4 Years

Are Uttaranchal University's Online Degrees equivalent with regular degrees?

Yes, As per UGC vide letter no. F.No.3-5/2022(DEB-III) and the subject of ""Equivalence of degree obtained through ODL and Online mode with degree obtained through conventional mode"" dated 2nd September, 2022.

All the Online Degrees offered by Uttaranchal University are equivalent to Regular Degrees.

How can I apply for the admission to Online UU?

Admission process is fully online and applicants are not required to visit personally anywhere. Visit www.onlineuu.online and click on the Apply Now button to initiate your online admission process. For any support, you can connect with the Program Advisors on 18002124454 or can send an email to admissions@onlineuu.online

Can I visit the campus and take the admission physically?

Yes, Candidates can visit the University campus on any working day from 9:30 AM to 4:30 PM and can complete the admission process. It is suggested to contact the University officials on 18002124454 before visiting the campus.

How do I know if I am eligible to take admission in the program?

Eligibility criteria for all the programs are specified on the respective program page of the website (www.onlineuu.online). Kindly visit the program page in which you are interested and check the eligibility to take admission.

You can also contact us at 18002124454 or send an email to admissions@onlineuu.online and we will assist you with the information.

Who can take admission in the Online UU programs?

Students, Professionals, Entrepreneurs, Housewives, or any other individual who wishes to pursue higher education.

What are the documents that I need to upload to the admission portal?

The scanned copies of the following documents are required at the time of filling up the application online:

  • Passport size photograph
  • 10th Marksheet
  • 12th Marksheet/Diploma Marksheets (of all Semesters)
  • Graduation Marksheets (of all semesters)/Degree Certificate (PG Programs)
  • Aadhaar card of the candidate (Both Sides)/Voter ID/PAN Card

International Candidates can upload their Passport for ID proof.

Can a student apply for the Program without the 10+2/Graduation final year result?

Yes, a student may apply provisionally for any program but needs to submit scanned copy of all the original Marksheets/ Degree within the date notified by the University.

In case you fail to submit the required documents, your admission will be cancelled by the University.

What is the maximum age at which one can take admission?

There is No Age limit to apply for admission in the Online UU Degree programs.

Am I eligible to pursue Ph.D. or any other higher education after acquiring a Degree from Online UU?

Yes, you are eligible to pursue Ph.D. or any other education after studying a degree from Online UU.

What is ULMS (University Lifecycle Management System)?

ULMS, is an exceptional and user-friendly Learning Management System (LMS) of Uttaranchal University. It provides the opportunity for the teaching-learning process at any time and location, establishing a robust and secure virtual connection between learners and the university.

It is a virtual platform that provides 24x7 accessibility through its various useful features at your fingertips.

When will I get the ULMS access?

Once your eligibility documents are accepted by the University, candidate will get the User ID and Password for the ULMS platform at the registered email id.
