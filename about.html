Who Are We?

Uttaranchal University has been recognized by the UGC under sections 2(f) and 12(B) of the UGC Act, 1956, and by other statutory bodies of the State and the Nation. The regular programs offered by Uttaranchal University and the emerging digital programs offered by Online Uttaranchal University (Online UU) are promoted by the Sushila Devi Centre for Professional Studies and Research, a society registered under the Societies Registration Act (1860).

The University has been accredited with the prestigious “A+ Grade” by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC). Uttaranchal University has become the first university in the State of Uttarakhand to receive an NAAC A+ Grade in the first cycle of Accreditation, thus creating history.

This firmly positions the University among the top 5% HEIs of the nation. The university is undeterred in its mission to provide the best experience to all its students whether regional, national or international, through quality teaching, investing in research and innovation, and inculcating the essence of the above Sanskrit Shloka in its students

Academic Structure & Governance of the University

The university is governed through central bodies, principally the Board of Governors, Board of Management, Planning Committee, Academic Council, and Finance Committee. These bodies include representatives from across the university that reflects decentralization and participative management, being functionary at the central level. These bodies have an overall responsibility for the administration of the university, planning its work, and managing its resources effectively.

The university is a confederation of Schools, Faculties, and Departments. The schools are governed by statutes and regulations passed by the University Grants Commission, the Government of India, and other mandatory approvals, as required from other Government Authorities.

Students live, eat and socialise in the University’s 12 schools, thus forming an administrative grouping of Faculties. There is a Student council in the university which includes representatives from respective schools on the board.

The schools covering different disciplines are Law, Technology, Management, Applied & Life Sciences, Liberal Arts, Agriculture, Pharmacy, Computing, Mass Communication & Journalism, Hotel & Hospitality Management, Nursing, and Health Sciences.

The university provides all officials with reasonable leadership and guidance, as well as the authority to work under well-defined policies and frameworks. Uttaranchal University has a well-defined organizational structure to promote a transparent and efficient decision-making process. Through stakeholder interaction with multiple authorities, committees, and bodies, the organizational structure is capable of assuring educational & societal effectiveness. The school representatives are an inevitable part of the university’s management and hold equity in the decision-making process in amalgamation with the central bodies of the university. Centres of studies are controlled by committees of management, bringing together representatives from several disciplines.

About

The university came into establishment in 2013, vide Uttaranchal University Act, 2012 (Uttarakhand Act. No. 11 of 2013) as a Private University. It’s located in Dehradun, the capital city of the Indian state of Uttarakhand. The Uttaranchal University has been recognized by the University Grants Commission, Government of India, under Sections 2(f) and 12(B) of the UGC Act, 1956, and other statutory bodies of the State and the nation.
